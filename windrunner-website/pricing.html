Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions

How does billing work?

WindrunnerRotations subscriptions are billed automatically either monthly or yearly, depending on your selected plan. You can cancel at any time from your account dashboard. Yearly subscriptions offer a 20% discount compared to paying monthly.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be billed the prorated difference immediately. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

How do I get started after subscribing?

After completing your subscription, you'll receive an email with download instructions and your license key. Simply download the WindrunnerRotations addon, place it in your WoW addons folder, start Tinkr, launch the game, and enter your license key in the settings panel.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all subscriptions. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.

Is WindrunnerRotations compatible with the latest WoW patch?

Yes! We pride ourselves on rapid updates following any game patches or class changes. Our team typically delivers updates within 24-48 hours of major game updates, ensuring you're never left behind.

How many characters can I use WindrunnerRotations on?

Bronze and Silver subscriptions allow usage on unlimited characters within a single WoW account. Gold subscribers get multi-account support for up to 3 separate WoW accounts under the same license.
